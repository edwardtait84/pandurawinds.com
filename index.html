About Pandura Winds

Pandura Winds are a woodwind trio based at the Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama, formed in 2026 by Harry Linegar, Alvaro Hurtado and Svetlana Nanda. They are well-known for their focus on contemporary music and innovative programming.
